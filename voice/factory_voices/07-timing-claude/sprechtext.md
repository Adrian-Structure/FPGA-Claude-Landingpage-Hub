# 07 - Timing Claude - Sprechtext (Voice-Over)

## Text

This is Timing Claude.
Here, precision becomes critical.

The design must meet timing.
Every path, every clock domain, and every nanosecond is inspected.

A design is not finished just because it works logically.
It must also work fast enough, reliably enough, and within timing limits.

Every nanosecond matters.

## Ausdruck / Ton

_(noch zu ergaenzen)_
